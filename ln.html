Leo/need

"A band formed by a group of childhood friends who have learned to overcome their differences."

The story of Leo/need is woven around their effort to reunite and overcome past differences to make music together again. Each member brings a unique voice and background to the group, adding layers of depth to their collective story. Ichika leads with her passion and determination, supported by Saki's cheerful energy, Honami's gentle kindness, and Shiho's strong will.

Leo/need's music reflects their journey, often focusing on themes of perseverance, dreams, and the power of friendship. Their songs resonate with fans for their heartfelt lyrics and catchy melodies, making Leo/need not just a musical group, but a symbol of enduring bonds and shared dreams.

Leo/need is composed of 4 members:

Hoshino Ichika (Vocalist)
Tenma Saki (Keyboardist)
Mochizuki Honami (Drummer)
Hinomori Shiho (Bassist)
