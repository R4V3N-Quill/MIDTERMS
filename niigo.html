Nightcord at 25:00

"A mysterious music circle that operates at 1:00 AM (25:00), and whose identities are shrouded in mystery."

Nightcord at 25:00 is an anonymous music unit. Their members remain anonymous, their identities hidden behind the veil of the internet.
The unit's name, "Nightcord at 25:00," refers to the late-night hour (1:00 am) when they are most active. Their music is often characterized by its melancholic and introspective tone, reflecting the themes of loneliness, isolation, and self-discovery that are explored in their songs.

Nightcord at 25:00 is composed of 4 members:

Yoisaki Kanade "K" (Composer)
Asahina Mafuyu "Yuki" (Lyricist)
Shinonome Ena "Enanan" (Illustrator)
Akiyama Mizuki "Amia" (MV Creator)

"K" formed the circle with the goal of creating music that "saves people" and gives them hope. She recruits "Enanan", "Amia", and "Yuki", they communicate via a chat tool called "Nightcord." None of the members knew each other's real names or appearance until later in the story.
