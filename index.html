Project SEKAI: Colorful Stage! feat. Hatsune Miku

Project SEKAI: Colorful Stage! feat. Hatsune Miku also known as Project SEKAI is a rhythm game that features songs from vocaloids, music covers, and original songs. Project SEKAI offers a variety of contents like, rich storytelling, 2dMV, and 3dMV that are fully customizable. Players can see their favorite character sing and dance their favorite song from other units because of this. Aside from the famous Vocaloids this game also has original characters that have diverse personalities.

The gameplay, like the other popular rhythm games requires the players to tap, swipe, and hold notes that appearon the screen. It requires good hand-eye coordination to effectively play the game. As players progress they will unlock new songs, stamps, and cards. These cards will unlock costumes for their characters and will help them progress into the game.

One aspect of the game that makes it stand out among the other rhythm games is the ability of players to attend live concerts in-game. During events there will be a live featuring the new song that is released in that event. Just like real life concerts players must wait for the given schedule of the game for them to attend these concerts. Aside from event lives theres also birthday lives, and Anniversary live.

Project SEKAI features an engaging storyline. Players will experience these stories through events, area conversations, and main story. The stories focus into details about the characters and their interactions in the real world and virtual worlds. The players will accompany these characters in their journey to self-discovery. While progressing in the story players will be able to understand the characters goals, motivations, behavior, and reasons.
