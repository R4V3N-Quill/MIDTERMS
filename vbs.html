Vivid Bad Squad

"A talented group of street artists who teamed up to surpass a legend."

Vivid Bad Squad story revolves around their shared goal of surpassing the legendary event RAD WEEKEND and rising to the top of the street music scene.
Each member of Vivid BAD SQUAD brings their own unique background and talent to the group Kohane is a passionate and talented musician, An is a street performer with a strong desire to succeed, Akito is a determined and hardworking member, and Toya is a shy but dedicated singer. Together, they form a powerful and inspiring team, driven by their common dream.

Vivid BAD SQUAD's music is characterized by its energetic and catchy tunes, often focusing on themes of ambition, perseverance, and the thrill of performing. Their songs and performances are designed to captivate and energize fans, making them a beloved unit within the game. Their journey from street musicians to aspiring legends is a central element of their story, resonating with fans who share their dreams and aspirations.

Vivid Bad Squad is composed of 4 members:
Azusawa Kohane
Shiraishi An
Shinonome Akito
Aoyagi Toya
