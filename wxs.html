Wonderlands x Showtime

"An eccentric musical show unit, proclaiming each day wonderful and rushing out to meet it head on."

Their story revolves around performing on the Wonder Stage in Phoenix Wonderland, where each day is proclaimed wonderful and met head-on with enthusiasm and energy.
Each member of Wonderlands x Showtime brings their own unique flair and personality to the group. Tsukasa is tvery confident and charismatic leader, Emu is a cheerful member, Nene is a shy but hardworking member, and Rui adds a touch of elegance and grace. Together, they create a dynamic and captivating team, driven by their love for the stage and their desire to entertain.

Wonderlands x Showtime's music is characterized by its upbeat and theatrical tunes, often drawing inspiration from musical theater.Their performances are bombastic and character-driven, designed to captivate and energize fans. Their journey from part-time actors to stars of the Wonder Stage is a central element of their story, resonating with fans who share their dreams and aspirations

Wonderlands x Showtime is composed of 4 members:
Tenma Tsukasa
Otori Emu
Kusanagi Nene
Kamishiro Rui
