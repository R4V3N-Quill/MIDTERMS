MORE MORE JUMP!

"A unique idol group formed by one girl with a dream, and three other girls who had given up on theirs."

More More Jump! is an energetic idol group. Their story revolves around the dream of becoming idols and the challenges they face along the way.
The members of More More Jump! each bring their own unique personalities and backgrounds to the group. Minori is the passionate leader with a strong desire to succeed, while Haruka, Airi, and Shizuku each have their own reasons for joining the group and overcoming their past struggles. Together, they form a dynamic and inspiring team.

More More Jump!'s music is characterized by its upbeat and catchy tunes, often focusing on themes of perseverance, friendship, and the joy of performing Their songs and performances are designed to uplift and energize fans, making them a beloved unit within the game Their journey from aspiring idols to successful performers is a central element of their story, resonating with fans who share their dreams and aspirations.

MORE MORE JUMP! is composed of 4 members:
Hanasato Minori
Kiritani Haruka
Momoi Airi
Hinomori Shizuku
